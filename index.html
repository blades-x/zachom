Economics

All products should have a circular life cycle based on the raw materials to end user product back again to usable raw materials.

Economic sustainability doesn’t stop there though. To increase the life of a product it must also be upgradable. This retains customers and allows for an additional sales opportunity without the development cost and end user cost of a new product.

2D Drafting Service

The modern age of 3D design has yield multiple advangates in providing easy to obtain more flexible designs, renders for marketing, interactive BOMs, and model simulations. This doesn't mean 2D design is dead though.

2D CAD drawings are still great for:

  • Conveying ideas without the cost of a full 3D work up
  • Site Layouts
  • Goverment Projects that require 2D drawings

Because of this, Zachom continues to offer these services.
